Our Two Pillars

Family-run. Community-rooted. Built for Omatjete.

Build: Shelter for Omatjete

Affordable homes, storage sheds, fences & roof repairs using local stone, clay bricks.

Pricing: N$150–250/hour labor (you supply materials)

Renovations & Repairs

Fix leaking roofs, cracked walls, or broken floors — quickly and fairly priced.

Payment: 30% upfront, 40% mid-way, 30% on completion

Bloom: Food for Omatjete

Fresh spinach, onions, tomatoes, chili peppers & indigenous greens — grown right here.

From N$15/bag — cheaper than Omaruru when you count fuel!

Local Produce Delivery

Door-to-door or pickup at our neighborhood stall. All labeled: “From Omatjete’s Soil”.

Weekly orders welcome!

Our Family, Our Legacy

We’re not an outside company — we’re your neighbors. Our kids play with yours. Our home is here.

How we work:

  • Train local youth as apprentices
  • Use rainwater harvesting & drip irrigation
  • Save 50% of profits to buy land in Omatjete
  • Keep a family journal of every milestone

We believe in no debt, no loans — just honest work, fair prices, and growing together.
